# Company Formation And Paid-Launch Admin

> **Status:** Planning checklist; not legal, tax, accounting, or investment advice
> **Last reviewed:** 2026-07-11 against the official Irish sources linked below
> **Source of truth for:** The project's company, ownership-record, banking, tax-registration, and paid-launch administrative sequence. Private founder terms do not belong in this repository.

Use a solicitor and accountant to apply these requirements to the founders and product. This document records a project order of work, not a conclusion about anyone's legal or tax position.

## Project Policy

A limited free technical beta may proceed before every company-administration task is complete if the user-data, terms, privacy, deletion, export, backup, and support gates in [launch-checklist.md](launch-checklist.md) are met.

Do not enable public paid subscriptions until the company-owned money path and ownership records are ready: incorporated entity, private founder/IP agreement, business banking, payment account, bookkeeping, and professional tax advice.

## Order Of Work

1. **Get professional advice.** Confirm the intended company type, ownership structure, director and secretary requirements, tax registrations, and treatment of existing work and expenses.
2. **Record founder and IP terms privately.** Cover ownership, intellectual-property assignment or licensing, decision-making, departures, spending authority, and historical expenses outside the public repository.
3. **Prepare incorporation details.** Agree the proposed name, registered office, officers, subscribers, and shares with professional guidance.
4. **Incorporate through CRO.** For an Irish LTD, the CRO currently directs applicants to submit Form A1 and a constitution through CORE.
5. **Build the company admin base.** Create company-controlled email, password management, document storage, bookkeeping, and recovery access.
6. **Record or transfer product assets.** Document company control or permitted use of the domain, code, designs, contracts, provider accounts, and other product assets.
7. **Open business banking and accounting records.** Keep company revenue and costs separate from personal transactions; have an accountant classify founder-paid expenses.
8. **Complete Revenue registrations and filings.** Confirm the applicable tax heads and deadlines with an accountant.
9. **Register beneficial ownership.** Identify the relevant natural persons with professional advice and file through the RBO portal by the applicable deadline.
10. **Open the payment account under company control.** Complete product review, payout banking, tax settings, receipts, refunds, and policy links.
11. **Finalise customer terms.** Publish reviewed terms, privacy, cookie, cancellation, and refund wording that matches the real product and providers.
12. **Enable paid checkout only after the paid-launch gates pass.** Keep receipts, invoices, contracts, and bookkeeping current from the first transaction.

## Verified Official Guidance

The following narrow statements were checked on 2026-07-11. Recheck the source and obtain professional advice before acting.

### Company registration

The [Companies Registration Office required steps](https://cro.ie/registration/company/required-steps/) say to submit Form A1 and a constitution through CORE. The page lists the company name, registered office, directors, secretary, subscribers, and share details among the Form A1 information.

### Revenue statement after trading begins

Revenue's [Company Statement of Particulars](https://www.revenue.ie/en/companies-and-charities/corporation-tax-for-companies/company-statement-of-particulars/index.aspx) page says an incorporated company or company beginning to trade in the State must provide a Statement of Particulars using Form 11F CRO within 30 days after it begins trading.

Do not infer every tax registration from this repository. Revenue lists Corporation Tax, employer registrations, and VAT among registrations available through eRegistration; an accountant should determine what applies and when.

### Beneficial ownership

The RBO's [beneficial-owner guidance](https://rbo.gov.ie/faqs/what-is-a-beneficial-owner-other-information/) says a newly incorporated relevant entity has five months from incorporation to register its beneficial ownership. It describes ownership or control above 25% as one indicator and also requires consideration of voting rights and control by other means.

Do not record assumptions about the actual founders or share split here. Determine and document the real beneficial owners with professional advice.

### VAT watchpoints

Revenue's current [VAT registration thresholds](https://www.revenue.ie/en/vat/vat-registration/who-should-register-for-vat/vat-thresholds.aspx) page lists EUR 42,500 for persons supplying services only. Its [electronically supplied services](https://www.revenue.ie/en/vat/vat-on-services/electronic-services/vat-and-electronically-supplied-services/index.aspx) guidance describes a conditional EUR 10,000 cross-border B2C TBE threshold and the place-of-supply and OSS consequences when the exception does not apply.

Whether OghmaNotes is an electronically supplied service for a particular transaction, how a Merchant of Record affects the analysis, and when registration is beneficial or required are professional-advice questions.

## Paid-Launch Evidence

Before closing the company/admin gate, retain private evidence that:

- incorporation and current officer details are recorded;
- founder and IP terms are signed or otherwise professionally documented;
- beneficial-ownership and Revenue actions are filed or scheduled against confirmed deadlines;
- business banking, payment payouts, receipts, refunds, and bookkeeping are company-controlled;
- domain, source control, Cloudflare, hosting, database, storage, email, AI, GPU, and payment accounts have resilient company access;
- final customer-facing policies have been reviewed and published.

The processor choice is recorded in [the payment decision](../decisions/2026-06-11-payment-processor.md). Product prices and entitlements belong only in [pricing.md](pricing.md).
