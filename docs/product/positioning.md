# Product Positioning

> **Status:** Current and reflected in the public site
> **Last reviewed:** 2026-07-11
> **Source of truth for:** Public product promise, message hierarchy, comparison framing, and claim boundaries.

## Core Promise

> Your whole semester, already loaded.

OghmaNotes is a Canvas-connected study workspace. Where a student's institution and permissions allow it, the product imports course material and deadlines so the student can move from setup to studying with less manual organisation.

The differentiator is not a generic chatbot. It is a study workspace grounded in the course the student is actually taking.

## Message Hierarchy

Lead with:

1. Connect Canvas and bring supported course material into one workspace.
2. Ask cited questions against indexed material.
3. Generate flashcards and review them with spaced repetition.
4. Track assignments and plan study time.
5. Export notes and files instead of being locked in.

Technical terms such as RAG, embeddings, vector search, OCR, and FSRS can support technical credibility later. They should not carry the main student-facing explanation.

## Honest Product Claims

It is accurate to say that OghmaNotes can:

- import supported Canvas courses, files, assignments, and deadlines where available;
- organise notes and uploaded course material;
- provide cited answers after content has been indexed;
- generate flashcards and support spaced-repetition review;
- provide assignment, time-block, and Pomodoro planning tools;
- import and export Markdown or Obsidian-style vaults.

Always qualify these constraints:

- Canvas access depends on the institution, account permissions, and available APIs.
- OCR and indexing can take minutes, particularly for scans or large histories.
- AI answers and generated study material can be wrong and should be checked.
- Import and AI usage have limits because processing has a real cost.
- OghmaNotes is independent and is not an official University of Galway or Canvas service.

Do not claim that OghmaNotes currently:

- automatically surfaces grades or rubric feedback;
- builds a complete exam revision plan automatically;
- imports every Canvas item or works at every institution;
- indexes large histories instantly;
- guarantees grades or replaces lecturers and official guidance;
- provides unlimited storage, unlimited AI, or free service forever.

## Primary Copy

Suggested hero:

> Your whole semester, already loaded.

Suggested explanation:

> Connect Canvas once. Bring your available lectures, deadlines, and course material into one study workspace for cited answers, flashcards, notes, and planning.

Suggested primary action:

> Connect Canvas free

The free action refers to the limited first-import experience defined in [pricing.md](pricing.md), not an unlimited entitlement.

## Comparison Framing

Use direct but respectful comparisons:

> NotebookLM is useful for documents you upload. OghmaNotes starts from the course you are actually taking.

Compare workflow and setup cost, not unverifiable quality or permanence claims. Competitor prices, limits, and feature claims must be rechecked before publication and should not live in this positioning document.

## Trust Rules

- Use real beta feedback only; never fabricate testimonials or scale.
- Say “built by students at University of Galway,” followed by the independence disclaimer.
- Keep public pricing aligned with [pricing.md](pricing.md).
- Remove or label unfinished features instead of presenting them as available.
- Preserve visible export, deletion, privacy, and contact paths.
- Keep machine-readable public descriptions aligned with the same claim boundaries.

The Canvas-first positioning was implemented on the public site in July 2026. Future edits should maintain it as a current messaging guide, not revive the earlier generic “AI notes app” framing.
