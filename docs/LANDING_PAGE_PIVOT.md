# Landing Page Positioning Pivot

Last updated: 2026-07-07

## Core Verdict

OghmaNotes should not be sold as another AI notes app. Students already have NotebookLM, ChatGPT, Gemini, Quizlet, Anki, and other free or cheap study tools. Competing on "AI chat", "RAG", "semantic notes", or "flashcards" makes the product look interchangeable.

The stronger product promise is:

> Your whole semester, already loaded.

OghmaNotes' moat is not that it is a smarter chatbot. It is that the study system assembles itself from the student's actual degree.

## Positioning

NotebookLM is an empty box: useful, but the student has to upload files, organise notebooks, add context, and keep it maintained.

OghmaNotes should lead with Canvas:

- lecture PDFs
- scanned slides
- deadlines
- grades and rubric feedback where available
- course structure
- exam and revision needs where available

The key promise:

> Connect Canvas once and your study system appears.

## Current Website Problem

The old public copy sold OghmaNotes as a generic AI study tool:

- "Semantic Notes & RAG Chat"
- upload PDFs
- cited AI answers
- quizzes and flashcards
- semantic search
- Markdown editor
- secure cloud storage
- collaboration
- multi-user support

Canvas was present, but treated as one feature among many instead of the main reason to care.

## New Landing Page Direction

Hero:

> Your whole semester, already loaded.

Subhead:

> Connect Canvas once. OghmaNotes pulls in your lectures, deadlines and course material, then turns them into cited answers, flashcards and an exam revision plan.

Primary CTA:

> Connect Canvas free

Secondary CTA:

> Compare with NotebookLM

## Feature Framing

Student-facing outcomes:

- Ask your lectures anything
- Flashcards that schedule themselves
- Never lose a deadline again
- Reads messy scanned slides
- Revision built around exams
- Export everything, no lock-in

Avoid leading with:

- RAG
- semantic notes
- embeddings
- OCR pipeline
- FSRS
- vector search

Those can appear later for technical credibility, but not as the primary student-facing pitch.

## NotebookLM Comparison

Use a direct, respectful comparison:

> NotebookLM is great for documents you upload. OghmaNotes is for the course you are actually taking.

The comparison should not attack NotebookLM. It should make the setup-removal advantage obvious.

## Pricing Pivot

Students are subscription-fatigued, so do not frame OghmaNotes as another monthly SaaS bill.

Lead with semester/year value:

- Free first import or one-module import
- Semester plan around EUR 39-49
- Academic-year plan around EUR 79-89
- Monthly pricing can exist, but should not be the main anchor

The free tier should include the magic moment. Do not paywall Canvas import before the student sees their semester appear. Keep limits honest because large imports have real OCR and AI costs.

## Trust Rules

- Remove fake testimonials.
- Replace with real beta quotes, founder notes, or transparent beta status.
- Say "built by students at University of Galway".
- Do not imply official University of Galway affiliation.
- Be clear that Canvas data availability depends on permissions and institution settings.
- Be clear that AI answers can be wrong.
- Give students export and deletion paths.

## Implementation Priorities

1. Rewrite homepage hero around Canvas import.
2. Replace RAG/semantic language with student outcomes.
3. Remove fake testimonials and fake scale claims.
4. Add "Why not NotebookLM?" section.
5. Reframe pricing around semesters and import costs.
6. Add LLM-readable public files.
7. Add a growth funnel doc before adding analytics code.
8. Keep public claims aligned with actual product and launch readiness.

## One-Line Strategy

Win by being the study tool students do not have to set up.

The magic moment is not chat. It is watching the semester assemble itself.
